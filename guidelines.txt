Guidelines:

Our guidelines ensure that the RideTogether community remains safe, respectful, and enjoyable for everyone. By using RideTogether, you agree to abide by the following guidelines:

Respect Others: Treat fellow users with kindness and respect.
Safety First: Prioritize safety while using RideTogether.
Honesty: Provide accurate information and be transparent in your interactions.
Follow Laws: Adhere to all local laws and regulations while using RideTogether.
Feedback: Provide constructive feedback to help us improve our services.
Report Issues: Report any issues or concerns to our support team immediately.
